# Applying to UK LLC
>Last modified: 21 May 2026
<div style="background-color: rgba(0, 178, 169, 0.3); padding: 5px; border-radius: 5px;"><strong>UK LLC welcomes applications from eligible researchers.</strong></div>
<br>

UK LLC is the national Trusted Research Environment (TRE) for data linkage in longitudinal research, bringing together data from Longitudinal Population Studies (LPS) and linking these to study participants’ health, administrative and place-based records. We welcome expressions of interest (EoIs) to conduct research projects. If eligible, both in terms of researcher and proposed project, you will be invited to submit a full application.
