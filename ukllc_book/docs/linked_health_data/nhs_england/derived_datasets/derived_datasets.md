# NHS England derived datasets
>Last modified: 18 Mar 2026
<div style="background-color: rgba(0, 178, 169, 0.3); padding: 5px; border-radius: 5px;"><strong>UK LLC has used pre-defined diagnostic codes to create a range of derived datasets based on NHS England data.</strong></div>
<br>

## DATAMIND collection
UK LLC has worked with <strong><a href="https://datamind.org.uk/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">DATAMIND</a></strong> to create derived datasets that cover a range of **mental health** and **neurodevelopmental conditions**. 

The conditions include:
- depression
- anxiety
- schizophrenia
- bipolar disorder
- autism spectrum disorder (ASD)
- attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder (ADHD). 

To see the full range of available datasets and to understand more, please read the [**DATAMIND collection guide**](../../../ukllc_managed_data/datasets/datamind/datamind_datasets.md).

## UK Biobank collection
UK LLC has generated derived datasets for **seven health outcomes**, partially based on algorithms developed and validated by <strong><a href="https://www.ukbiobank.ac.uk/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">UK Biobank</a></strong>. 

The conditions are:
- asthma
- chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD)
- dementia
- myocardial infarction (MI)
- motor neurone disease (MND)
- Parkinson's disease
- stroke. 

To understand more, please read the [**UK Biobank collection guide**](../../../ukllc_managed_data/datasets/biobank/ukbb_datasets.md).
